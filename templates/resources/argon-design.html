Argon Design is a modern, open-source design system created by Creative Tim that provides a comprehensive set of components and templates for building web applications. It's known for its sleek aesthetic and professional appearance, making it popular for dashboards, admin panels, and business applications.


Argon Design strikes a balance between visual appeal and professional functionality, making it a popular choice for developers and designers building business-oriented web applications that need to convey trustworthiness and professionalism.
