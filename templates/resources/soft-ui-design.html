Soft UI (also known as Neumorphism) is a design trend that emerged as an evolution of flat design and skeuomorphism. It creates a soft, extruded plastic look with subtle shadows and highlights to give elements a tactile, almost physical presence on the screen without being overly three-dimensional.


Soft UI represents a unique approach to digital design that creates a sense of physical interaction in an increasingly flat digital world, blending the tactile feeling of physical objects with the clean simplicity of modern interfaces.
